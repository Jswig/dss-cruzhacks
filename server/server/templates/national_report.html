An overview of the political ad money propogating through Facebook.

Any advertiser's Facebook ads come in two parts: how many times they pay to have their ads served, and the number of unique ads that they choose to serve. In 2016, Facebook played an important role in determining many voter's political opionions through ad campaigns. Therefore, it is an important part of our democratic process to see where the money is going.
